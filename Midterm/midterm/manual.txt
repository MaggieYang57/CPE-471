Pressing 'T'/'G' - moves left arm up/down
Pressing 'Y'/'H' - moves right arm up/down
Pressing 'R'/'F' - moves left leg forward/back
Pressing 'U'/'J' - moves right leg forward/back
Pressing 'B' - moves all limbs repeatedly. Pressing it again stops animation.

w/a/s/d - controls camera
